Drinks & Smoothies

Mango Lassi

Ingredients:

1 ripe mango, peeled and diced 1 cup yogurt 1/2 cup milk (adjust for consistency) 2-3 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste) 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder Ice cubes (optional) Fresh mint leaves (for garnish)

Instructions:

Blend Ingredients: In a blender, add diced mango, yogurt, milk, sugar, and cardamom powder. Blend Until Smooth: Blend everything until smooth and creamy. You can adjust the consistency by adding more milk if needed. Taste and Adjust: Taste the lassi and adjust the sweetness or cardamom powder as desired. Add Ice: If you like it chilled, add ice cubes to the blender and blend again. Serve: Pour into glasses and garnish with mint leaves. Serve immediately.


Masala Chai (Spiced Tea)

Ingredients:

2 cups water 1 cup milk 2 teaspoons tea leaves (black tea) 1-2 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste) 1-2 green cardamom pods, crushed 1 small piece of ginger, grated 2-3 cloves (optional) 1 small cinnamon stick (optional)

Instructions:

Boil Water and Spices: In a saucepan, add water, crushed cardamom pods, grated ginger, cloves, and cinnamon stick. Bring to a boil. Add Tea Leaves: Once the water is boiling, add tea leaves and let it simmer for 2-3 minutes. Add Milk: Pour in the milk and bring the mixture back to a boil. Add sugar and stir well. Strain the Tea: Turn off the heat and strain the tea into cups using a fine mesh strainer. Serve: Serve hot, optionally with some biscuits or snacks.


Coconut Water Smoothie

Ingredients:

1 cup coconut water 1 banana 1/2 cup yogurt (optional) 1 tablespoon honey (optional) Ice cubes (optional) Fresh mint leaves (for garnish)

Instructions:

Prepare Ingredients: Gather all the ingredients. If using yogurt, it should be chilled. Blend: In a blender, combine coconut water, banana, yogurt (if using), and honey. You can add ice cubes if you prefer a chilled smoothie. Blend Until Smooth: Blend until the mixture is smooth and creamy. Taste and Adjust: Taste the smoothie and adjust the sweetness by adding more honey if desired. Serve: Pour into glasses and garnish with mint leaves. Serve immediately.


Thandai (Spiced Milk Drink)

Ingredients:

2 cups milk (chilled) 1/4 cup almonds (soaked and peeled) 1/4 cup cashews (soaked) 2-3 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste) 1-2 green cardamom pods 1/2 teaspoon black pepper 1 tablespoon rose water Ice cubes (optional) Chopped nuts and rose petals (for garnish)

Instructions:

Prepare Nut Paste: In a blender, combine soaked almonds, cashews, sugar, cardamom pods, black pepper, and a little milk. Blend into a smooth paste. Mix with Milk: In a large bowl, combine the nut paste with the remaining chilled milk and rose water. Stir well. Chill: If you want a cooler drink, you can refrigerate the mixture for about 30 minutes. Serve: Pour into glasses. If desired, add ice cubes for a chilled drink. Garnish: Garnish with chopped nuts and rose petals before serving.


Spiced Buttermilk (Chaas)

Ingredients:

1 cup yogurt 2 cups water 1/2 teaspoon roasted cumin powder 1/2 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste) 1-2 green chilies, chopped (optional) Fresh coriander leaves, chopped Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions:

Whisk Yogurt: In a bowl, whisk the yogurt until smooth. You can use a blender for a smoother consistency. Add Water: Gradually add water to the yogurt and mix well until you reach your desired consistency. Add Spices: Stir in roasted cumin powder, salt, and chopped green chilies (if using). Mix well. Chill: You can add ice cubes to the buttermilk for a refreshing drink or refrigerate for a while. Serve: Pour into glasses and garnish with chopped coriander leaves. Serve chilled.
