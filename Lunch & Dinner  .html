Lunch & Dinner

Jeera Rice (Cumin Rice)

Ingredients:

1 cup basmati rice 1 tbsp ghee or oil 1 tsp cumin seeds 1.5 cups water Salt to taste

Instructions:

Rinse the rice thoroughly, then soak it for 15 minutes and drain. Heat ghee or oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter. Add rice, sauté for a minute, then add water and salt. Cover and cook on low heat until rice is fully cooked (about 10–12 minutes). Fluff with a fork and serve with curries or dal.


Aloo Gobi (Potato and Cauliflower Curry)

Ingredients:

2 medium potatoes (cubed) 1 small cauliflower (cut into florets) 1 tbsp oil 1 tsp cumin seeds 1 tsp turmeric powder 1 tsp coriander powder Salt to taste

Instructions:

Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them sizzle. Add potatoes, cauliflower, turmeric, and coriander powder; mix well. Cover and cook on low heat until potatoes and cauliflower are tender. Season with salt, stir, and cook for a few more minutes. Serve with roti or rice.


Palak Paneer (Spinach with Cottage Cheese)

Ingredients:

2 cups fresh spinach leaves 200g paneer (cubed) 1 tbsp oil or butter 1 small onion (chopped) 1 tomato (chopped) 1 tsp cumin seeds Salt to taste

Instructions:

Blanch spinach in boiling water for 2 minutes, then blend to make a puree. Heat oil or butter, add cumin seeds, then add onions and sauté until golden. Add tomatoes, cook until soft, then add the spinach puree. Add paneer cubes, season with salt, and simmer for 5 minutes. Serve hot with naan or roti.


Chana Masala (Spiced Chickpea Curry)

Ingredients:

1 cup canned or boiled chickpeas 1 tbsp oil 1 small onion (chopped) 1 tomato (chopped) 1 tsp cumin seeds 1 tsp chana masala powder (or garam masala) Salt to taste

Instructions:

Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, then add onions and sauté until golden. Add tomatoes, cook until soft, then add chana masala powder. Add chickpeas, salt, and a little water; cook for 5–7 minutes. Mash a few chickpeas for a thicker consistency if desired. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve with rice or bread.


Bhindi Masala (Okra Stir-Fry)

Ingredients:

250g okra (bhindi), cut into small pieces 1 tbsp oil 1 tsp cumin seeds 1/2 tsp turmeric powder 1/2 tsp coriander powder Salt to taste

Instructions:

Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them crackle. Add okra pieces, turmeric, and coriander powder; mix well. Cook on medium heat, stirring occasionally, until okra is tender and slightly crispy. Season with salt and cook for a few more minutes. Serve with roti or rice.
