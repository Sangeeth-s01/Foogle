Breakfast & Brunch 1. Aloo Paratha Stuffed flatbread made with spiced mashed potatoes and cooked on a griddle, served with yogurt and pickle. 2. Masala Dosa A crispy rice and lentil crepe filled with spiced potato filling, served with coconut chutney and sambar. 3. Poha Flattened rice cooked with spices, turmeric, peanuts, onions, and garnished with coriander and lemon juice. 4. Upma A savory semolina dish flavored with mustard seeds, curry leaves, green chilies, and vegetables. 5. Chole Bhature Spicy chickpea curry served with deep-fried bread, often accompanied by pickles and onions. 2. Lunch & Dinner 1. Butter Chicken Creamy, mildly spiced chicken cooked in a rich tomato-based gravy with butter and cream. 2. Paneer Butter Masala Cottage cheese cubes simmered in a creamy, spicy tomato sauce, flavored with butter and spices. 3. Biryani Aromatic basmati rice layered with marinated chicken, mutton, or vegetables and spices, then cooked together. 4. Dal Tadka Yellow lentils cooked with spices, topped with a flavorful tempering of garlic, onions, and tomatoes. 5. Rogan Josh A Kashmiri-style curry made with lamb or goat, cooked in a yogurt-based sauce with Kashmiri chilies and spices. 3. Snacks & Appetizers 1. Samosa Deep-fried pastry filled with spiced potatoes and peas, served with tamarind and mint chutneys. 2. Pakoras (Vegetable Fritters) Assorted vegetables coated in a chickpea flour batter and deep-fried until crispy, served with green chutney. 3. Pav Bhaji A spicy mashed vegetable curry served with buttery toasted buns, garnished with onions and lemon. 4. Dahi Puri Hollow crispy puris filled with spiced potatoes, yogurt, and chutneys, topped with sev and coriander. 5. Aloo Tikki Pan-fried potato patties spiced with green chilies and coriander, served with chutney or as a chaat with toppings. 4. Desserts & Sweet Treats 1. Gulab Jamun Fried milk-solid dumplings soaked in a fragrant sugar syrup flavored with cardamom and rosewater. 2. Jalebi Deep-fried batter in spiral shapes, soaked in saffron-infused sugar syrup, and enjoyed hot or cold. 3. Kheer A creamy rice pudding made with milk, sugar, and aromatic spices like cardamom, garnished with nuts. 4. Rasgulla Spongy, syrupy balls made from chenna (Indian cottage cheese) soaked in light sugar syrup. 5. Besan Ladoo Sweet balls made with roasted chickpea flour, ghee, and sugar, flavored with cardamom. 5. Healthy & Special Diets 1. Moong Dal Chilla Protein-packed savory pancakes made from ground moong dal (green gram) and flavored with spices and veggies. 2. Palak Paneer A nutrient-rich curry made with spinach puree and cottage cheese, seasoned with spices and garlic. 3. Raita A refreshing yogurt-based side dish with cucumber, carrots, or boondi, seasoned with spices and herbs. 4. Quinoa Pulao A healthy, protein-rich pulao made with quinoa, vegetables, and spices, perfect for a light meal. 5. Sprout Salad A crunchy and nutritious salad with sprouted lentils, chopped veggies, lemon juice, and chaat masala. 6. Drinks & Smoothies 1. Masala Chai Spiced tea made with black tea, milk, sugar, and spices like ginger, cardamom, and cloves. 2. Mango Lassi A refreshing yogurt-based mango smoothie, flavored with a touch of cardamom and saffron. 3. Aam Panna A tangy summer drink made from green mango pulp, mint, and spices, served chilled. 4. Buttermilk (Chaas) A refreshing and digestive drink made from diluted yogurt, cumin, and salt, often garnished with coriander. 5. Coconut Water & Lime Cooler Fresh coconut water blended with a splash of lime juice and mint leaves, perfect for hydration.
