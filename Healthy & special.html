Quinoa Upma

Ingredients:

1 cup quinoa 1 onion, chopped 1 carrot, chopped 1/2 cup peas 2 green chilies, chopped Mustard seeds, curry leaves, salt, and lemon juice to taste

Instructions:

Rinse quinoa and cook it in 2 cups of water until fluffy. Set aside. In a pan, heat oil and add mustard seeds. Once they crackle, add curry leaves. Sauté onions, green chilies, carrot, and peas until soft. Add cooked quinoa and salt; mix well. Squeeze lemon juice on top before serving.


Chickpea Salad

Ingredients:

1 can chickpeas (or 1 cup cooked chickpeas) 1 cucumber, diced 1 tomato, diced 1 onion, chopped Fresh coriander leaves, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to taste

Instructions:

In a large bowl, combine chickpeas, cucumber, tomato, and onion. Add chopped coriander, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Toss well to mix all ingredients. Serve chilled or at room temperature.


Palak Dal (Spinach Lentil Curry)

Ingredients:

1 cup yellow lentils (toor dal or moong dal) 2 cups spinach, chopped 1 onion, chopped 2 tomatoes, chopped 2 green chilies, slit Turmeric, cumin seeds, salt, and oil

Instructions:

Cook lentils in water with turmeric until soft. In a pan, heat oil, add cumin seeds and onions. Sauté until golden. Add tomatoes and green chilies; cook until tomatoes soften. Add chopped spinach and cook until wilted. Mix in the cooked lentils, add salt, and simmer for a few minutes.


Oats Dosa

Ingredients:

1 cup oats 1/2 cup yogurt 1/2 cup water 1 onion, finely chopped Green chilies, ginger, salt, and oil for cooking

Instructions:

Blend oats into a fine powder. Mix oat flour with yogurt, water, chopped onion, green chilies, ginger, and salt to make a batter. Heat a non-stick pan and pour a ladle of batter, spreading it into a circle. Drizzle oil around the edges and cook until golden; flip and cook the other side. Serve with chutney or sambar.


Vegetable Pulao

Ingredients:

1 cup basmati rice 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans) 1 onion, sliced 2 green chilies, slit Whole spices (cinnamon, cloves, cardamom), salt, and oil

Instructions:

Rinse basmati rice and soak for 30 minutes. Drain. In a pressure cooker, heat oil and sauté whole spices and onions until golden. Add mixed vegetables and sauté for a few minutes. Add the drained rice and water (2 cups), along with salt. Stir gently. Pressure cook for 1 whistle; let the steam release naturally before serving.
